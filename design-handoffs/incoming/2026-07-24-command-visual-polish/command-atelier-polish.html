MOTION RULES
· Durations 150–420ms; ease-out for entrances, ease for state.
· Exactly one ambient loop (active-step breathe), nowhere else.
· Failure states never loop — one shake, then rest.
· Everything gated by prefers-reduced-motion + the header switch.
· Historical / non-live threads render with .frozen — zero motion.
WHAT NEVER MOVES
· No progress bar without a real backend step event.
· No spinner implying work that isn't happening.
· No count-up numbers on invented totals.
· No gradient sweeps behind body text.
· No decorative particle / glow ambience.
