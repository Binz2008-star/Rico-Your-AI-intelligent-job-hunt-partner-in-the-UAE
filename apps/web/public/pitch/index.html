Folio 02Rico AI — Investor ProspectusThe Problem

§ The problem

The market is loud.
The tools are louder.

The UAE runs on newcomers — an expat-majority workforce for whom finding work is the path to residency, stability, and a life. Yet the search itself is impersonal, noisy, and built for volume, not for people.

01

Noise, no guidance

Hundreds of near-identical listings and no one to make sense of them. People apply to role after role before a single reply — and never learn why.

02

Generic help doesn't fit

The big AI tools are built for the whole world. They don't know UAE visa rules, real salary ranges, or how a Dubai recruiter actually reads a CV.

03

Rejected before a human reads it

UAE hiring runs on locally-formatted CVs, and screening software turns most people away before a person ever sees their name.

04

One language, many people

200+ nationalities, two working languages. Almost every tool speaks English only — leaving people who live in Arabic, or a mix of both, unserved.

Folio 04Rico AI — Investor ProspectusThe Product

§ The product

Not a job board.
A career partner.

One quiet voice that learns you, watches the market, and speaks only with reason — delivered where the UAE workforce already lives: Telegram and the web.

A

Learns you

It reads your CV, then learns more as you chat — your goals, your no-gos, the salary you need, the city you want.

B

Watches the market

Rico follows UAE job sources around the clock and ranks how well each one fits you. Only the strong matches ever reach you.

C

Explains itself

Every match opens up: how well it fits, why it fits in plain words, and the one thing worth knowing before you apply.

D

Drafts, never fakes

Honest, UAE-localized cover letters and CV tailoring. Rico strengthens the truth — it never invents experience.

E

Keeps the thread

An application ledger, follow-up reminders, and status tracking so nothing you started quietly disappears.

F

Speaks both tongues

English, Arabic, and the code-switched middle. Built first for the UAE's polyglot reality, not retrofitted for it.
