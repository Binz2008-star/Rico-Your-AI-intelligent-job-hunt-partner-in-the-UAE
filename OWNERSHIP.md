# Ownership

Rico Hunt / Rico AI is created, designed, owned, and directed by Roben Edwan.

Roben is the product owner and final decision-maker for product vision, AI behavior, UX direction, roadmap priority, safety boundaries, release readiness, and production acceptance.

## Direction

Rico is a UAE-focused AI career companion. It helps job seekers understand their profile, discover realistic job opportunities, receive explainable matches, prepare honest applications, track progress, and stay in control of important actions.

## Governance

Contributors and automation tools must preserve the existing Rico architecture, avoid parallel systems without approval, keep user actions permission-based, and avoid claiming production readiness without test and smoke evidence.

## Release standard

A change is production-ready only after the applicable tests, build, deployment verification, and smoke checks pass.
